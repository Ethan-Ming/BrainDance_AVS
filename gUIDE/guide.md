 ## 连接RGB LED
首先确认4个引脚位置
 ![PXL_20240224_024029675~2](https://github.com/Ethan-Ming/BrainDance_AVS/assets/36059080/3b48ac52-fbc2-4509-8e08-eaf8ec5a32f8)
